## Independent Reading Project

For the **Spring Independent Reading Project**, you will select a work of American literature published after 1960. Your project must include:

1. A 750-word critical analysis
2. A creative response component (format of your choice — visual, written, or multimedia)
3. A 5-minute class presentation

A full rubric is available in Google Classroom under *Assignments → Spring IRP*.
