## Photo Submission Guidelines

1. **File format:** JPG or PNG only
2. **Minimum resolution:** 300 DPI for print
3. **File naming:** `LastName_FirstName_Description.jpg` (e.g., `Smith_Jordan_SoccerGame.jpg`)
4. **Upload location:** Google Drive → Yearbook 2025 → [Your Section]
5. Do **not** use screenshots from social media — image quality is too low for print
